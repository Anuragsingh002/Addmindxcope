About Mindxcope

Mindxcope is an innovative AI-driven platform that analyzes cognitive signals like typing behavior, brain activity (EEG), and stress metrics to evaluate focus, creativity, and decision-making ability — enabling companies to hire smarter.

Typing & Browsing Behavior

AI-Powered Insights

Get predictive hiring recommendations based on cognitive data, not just resumes.
